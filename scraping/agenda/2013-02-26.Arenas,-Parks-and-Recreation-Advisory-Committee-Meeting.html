3.            Planner, Urban Design

Report APRAC13-009

Presentation of Louis Street Urban Park Site Plan with UN Peacekeepers Public Art Monument Location

 

A report to introduce a presentation by the Planner, Urban Design and the Cultural Planner on the Louis Street Urban Park site plan and the location of a UN Peacekeepers Public Art Monument, and to seek the Arenas, Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee's (APRAC) endorsement of the plan.

Presentation of the Trans-Canada Trail at the Canadian Pacific Railway (CPR) Bridge Project

5.         Manager, Facilities & Special Projects

Report APRAC 13-007

Sport Field Construction Update

 

A report to provide an update in the implementation and construction of new and improved sport field projects.

 

6.         Manager, Facilities & Special Projects

Report APRAC13-008

Water Play Park and Splash Pad Project Updates

 

A report to provide an update on the Rogers Cove Water Play Park and the Nicholls Oval Splash Pad projects.

 

7.         Arena Division Manager

Report APRAC13-006

Canteen at Northcrest Arena

 

A report to inform the Arena, Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee on the closure of the canteen at Northcrest Arena.

 

8.         Recreation Division Coordinator

Report APRAC13-004

City Sponsored Events and Activities

 

A report to request specific community events and activities be designated as City Sponsored, and be covered under the City's insurance.

 

9.         Recreation Division Coordinator

Report APRAC13-005

2013 Special Occasion Permits

 

A report to request that the Arenas, Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee endorse the issuance of Special Occasion Permits for 2013 Public Events, that do not require a Municipal Designation, and are occurring on City property.
