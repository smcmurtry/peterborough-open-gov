Director of Community Services Report PPL16-023 Development of a Memorandum of Understanding Between the Library Board and City

Ken Doherty, Director of Community Services, provided a review of Report PPL16-023.

Moved by Mauro DiCarlo

That the Peterborough Public Library Board of Trustees approve the recommendation outlined in Report PPL16-023 dated May 31, 2016 of the Director of Community Services, as follows:

That a joint Library Board/City Committee, comprised of seven members including three Board representatives (Eric Prevost, Teresa Kerr, and Leslie Woolcott), three City representatives (Ken Doherty, Director of Community Services; Sandra Clancy, Director of Corporate Services and Patricia Lester, City Solicitor), and the Library Manager/CEO, be established.

“CARRIED”

Moved by Mauro DiCarlo

That the Peterborough Public Library Board of Trustees approve the recommendation outlined in Report PPL16-023 dated May 31, 2016 of the Director of Community Services, as follows:

That the Committee be charged with the responsibility of drafting a letter of intent and making a recommendation about the appointment of a Treasurer for the Board’s approval at the June 28, 2016 meeting;

Developing a draft Memorandum of Understanding/Service Agreement for Board and City approval before the end of 2016; and,

Developing an updated Establishment By-law for the Board and Council’s consideration.

“CARRIED”
