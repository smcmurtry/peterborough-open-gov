Discussion Regarding the Municipal Relationship with the Board

Eric Prevost provided introductory comments on research he has undertaken on the relationship between the Library and the City.

Ken Doherty, Director of Community Services, provided the Board with the history of the Library and the City.

Moved by Eric Prevost

That the Library Manager/CEO prepare a report on the nature of relationships between municipalities and libraries in Ontario; and,

That the Director of Community Services provide information on the structure of the relationship between the Peterborough Public Library Board of Trustees and the City of Peterborough; and

That this report come forward in the first quarter of 2016.

“CARRIED”
