Director of Utility Services, City of Peterborough Report WMC17-001 2017 Waste Management Committee Meeting Schedule

Moved by Councillor Vassiliadis

That the Waste Management Committee approve the recommendation as outlined in Report WMC17-001 dated January 23, 2017, of the Director of Utility Services as follows:

That the following 2017 meeting dates for the Waste Management Committee be approved:

Monday, January 23, 2017 Monday, March 13, 2017 Monday, May 8, 2017 Monday, June 19, 2017 Monday, September 18, 2017 Monday, November 6, 2017

“CARRIED”

Director of Utility Services, City of Peterborough Report WMC17-002 Increase in Peterborough County/City Waste Management Facility Property Rental Rates

Moved by Councillor Vassiliadis

That the Waste Management Committee endorse the recommendation as outlined in Report WMC17-002 dated January 23, 2017 of the Director of Utility Services, as follows:

That the land rental rates for Peterborough County/City Waste Management Facility lands be increased from $40 per acre to $45 per acre for the period January 1, 2018 to December 31, 2021.

“CARRIED”

Director of Utility Services, City of Peterborough Report WMC17-003 Implementation of a Fine for Not Weighing Out at the Peterborough County/City Waste Management Facility

Moved by Councillor Pappas

That the Waste Management Committee endorse the recommendation outlined in Report WMC17-003 dated January 23, 2017, of the Director of Utility Services, as follows:

That a fine of $15.00 be levied on persons who do not weigh out at the Peterborough County/City Waste Management Facility effective January 1, 2017.


“CARRIED”
