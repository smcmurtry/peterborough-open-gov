Other Business

Wayne Jackson, Acting Chief Administrative Officer, updated Committee on Report WMC15-008 – Award of Proposal P-06-15 for the Transportation and Processing of Landfill Recyclables from Peterborough Waste Management Facilities. An email was circulated to Committee members for endorsement on April 14, 2015 and all members were in favour of the recommendations outlined in Report WMC15-008 (as below).

That the Waste Management Committee approve the recommendations as outlined in Report WMC15-008 dated April 14, 2015, of the Director of Utility Services as follows:

a)

That City RFP #P-06-15 for the Transportation and Processing of Landfill Recyclables from Peterborough Waste Management Facilities be awarded as a two-year contract beginning May 16, 2015 until May 15, 2017 with an option to extend the contract under the same terms and conditions for two additional one year terms, upon mutual agreement by the City and the Contractor, to:

i)

Peterborough Iron and Metal Inc., 40 Erskine Avenue, Peterborough, Ontario K9J 5T9 for the transportation, processing and supply of roll-off depot bins for scrap metal at a revenue of $180.00 per tonne, exclusive of applicable taxes, or an estimated revenue of $58,680.00 per year.
