Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC15-028 Heritage Designation 252 Parkhill Road

Moved by Councillor Pappas

That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report PACAC15-028, dated September 3, 2015 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

That 252 Parkhill Road (Ashley) be recommended to Council for designation pursuant to Part IV, Section 29 of the Ontario Heritage Act as being of cultural heritage value or interest.

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC15-025 Impact of Community Mailboxes on Heritage Properties

Moved by Councillor Pappas

That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report PACAC15-025, dated September 3, 2015 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

That the report about the potential impact of the installation of community mailboxes by Canada Post on, or in proximity to, individual heritage properties and heritage conservation districts be received for discussion.

“CARRIED”
