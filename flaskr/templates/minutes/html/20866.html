Director of Utility Services, City of Peterborough Report WMC15-011 Increase in Tipping Fee for Drywall – Update Report

Moved by Councillor Baldwin

That the Waste Management Committee approve the recommendations as outlined in Report WMC15-011 dated September 21, 2015, of the Director of Utility Services as follows:

That the tipping fee for drywall received at the Peterborough County/City Waste Management Facility be increased from $45/tonne to $90/tonne effective January 1, 2016; and

That staff be requested to take whatever actions are necessary to implement Recommendation A.

“CARRIED”

Director of Utility Services, City of Peterborough Report WMC15-012 Increase in Tipping Fee for Contaminated Soil – Update Report

Moved by Councillor Baldwin

That the Waste Management Committee approve the recommendations as outlined in Report WMC15-012 dated September 21, 2015, of the Director of Utility Services as follows:

That the tipping fee for contaminated soil received at the Peterborough County/City Waste Management Facility be increased from $20/tonne to $40/tonne effective January 1, 2016; and

That staff be requested to take whatever actions are necessary to implement Recommendation A.
