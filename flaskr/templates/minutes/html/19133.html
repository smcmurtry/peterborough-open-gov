Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC14-004 Martha Kidd Commemoration

Moved by Martin Parker

That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC14-004, dated January 15, 2014 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

a)

That $10,150 (including $4,500 being pre-committed from 2013 budget, $1,000 of proceeds from the sale of Beneath The Canopy, $450 from the sale of PACAC merchandise, $3,500 from the 2014 special projects budget and $700 reallocation of 2013 special projects budget) be allocated for the production of a publication on heritage preservation in Peterborough to honour the life and work of Martha Ann Kidd, and;

That the PACAC make the publication a collection of edited essays instead of a walking tour brochure as previously proposed.

b)

Committee at its meeting of January 15, 2014 added the following:

c)

That PACAC undertake a fundraising campaign to support the completion of the Martha Kidd commemoration project.

“CARRIED”
