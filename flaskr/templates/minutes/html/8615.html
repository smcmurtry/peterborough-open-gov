Minutes of a Meeting of Arts, Culture & Heritage Advisory Committee

Held on January 9, 2014, in the Sutherland Room, City Hall


The meeting of the Arts, Culture & Heritage Advisory Committee was called to order at 7:04 p.m. by Kendra Sedgwick, Committee Coordinator in the Sutherland Room, City Hall.

Present: Dennis Carter-Edwards Councillor Pappas Councillor Parnell Michael Bell Fynn Leitch Bruce Stonehouse

Regrets: Laurel Atkinson

Staff: Becky Rogers, Manager of Arts, Culture and Heritage Ken Doherty, Director of Community Services Erik Hanson, Heritage Resources Coordinator Debra Soule, Municipal Cultural Planner Kendra Sedgwick, Committee Coordinator

Election of Officers

The Committee Coordinator called for nominations for the position of Chair.

Moved by Councillor Pappas

That Dennis Carter-Edwards be appointed as Chair of the Arts, Culture and Heritage Advisory Committee for 2014.

“CARRIED”

Dennis Carter-Edwards assumed the Chair.
