Peterborough County/City Paramedics Service (PCCP) Preliminary Draft 2019 Budget

Moved by Henry Clarke Seconded by Councillor Smith

That the Joint Services Steering Committee (JSSC) receive the preliminary draft 2019 PCCP budget proposal and approve it to be forwarded to the City of Peterborough to act as a preliminary estimate to assist the City in their 2019 budget development; AND

That the JSSC recommend that Scenario 2 be used for the purposes of drafting the 2019 PCCP budget, where Scenario 2 includes a service enhancement converting the existing 6-month Buckhorn seasonal ambulance coverage to permanent full-year coverage with the addition of 6 new FTEs.

Carried
