Minutes of a Meeting of City Council Held on November 14, 2011

in the Council Chambers, City Hall


Meeting called to order at 7:00 p.m. in the Council Chambers.

Roll Call: Councillor Beamer Councillor Clarke Councillor Doris Councillor Juby Councillor Hall Councillor McWilliams Councillor Pappas Councillor Parnell Councillor Riel Councillor Vass Mayor Bennett

Ceremonial Presentations:

2011 Prince of Wales Prize for Municipal Heritage Leadership Natalie Bull, Executive Director of the Heritage Canada Foundation presented the City with the 2011 Prince of Wales Prize for Municipal Heritage Leadership.

Mayor’s Youth Awards Councillor Beamer provided an introduction to the awards. The recipients of the Mayor’s Youth Awards were Chen Rao and Richelia Giardino.

Canada World Youth Councillor Beamer introduced the award and congratulated the youth organization for their efforts.

Confirmation of Minutes – October 24, 2011

Moved by Councillor Clarke, seconded by Councillor Pappas

That the minutes of the meeting of Council held on October 24, 2011, be approved.
