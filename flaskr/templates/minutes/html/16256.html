Minutes of a Meeting of the Councillor Compensation Committee held

on September 1, 2016 in the Sutherland Room


The meeting Councillor Compensation Committee was called to order at 3:00 p.m. in the Sutherland Room, City Hall.

Present: Claude Dufresne Wayne Godward Daniel Kivari (3:02 p.m.) Dave McFadden Dan Moloney Garth Wedlock Jeff Westlake

Staff: Natalie Garnett, Deputy City Clerk John Kennedy, City Clerk

Disclosure of Pecuniary Interest

There were no disclosures of interest.

Committee members and staff introduced themselves.

Selection of Chair

The Clerk called for nominations for the position of Chair.

Moved by Claude Dufresne

That Garth Wedlock be appointed Chair of the Council Compensation Committee.

Mr. Wedlock accepted the nomination and there were no further nominations.

“CARRIED”
