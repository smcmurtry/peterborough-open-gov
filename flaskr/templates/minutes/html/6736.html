“CARRIED”

Disclosure of Pecuniary Interest

Recreation Division Coordinator Report APRAC16-006 Vision 2025 Project Update

Moved by David Ronson

That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report APRAC16-006 dated February 23, 2016, of the Recreation Division Coordinator, as follows:

a)

That a presentation by Mr. Robert Lockhart, from RETHINK Group, on the preliminary findings of the Vision 2025 Consultation activities conducted in 2015 be received; and

b)

That the Rules of Order be suspended to permit members of the Arts Culture and Heritage Advisory Committee, Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee, Museum and Archives Advisory Committee, and the Accessibility Advisory Committee to ask questions and provide input, following the Vision 2025 presentation from Mr. Lockhart.

“CARRIED”

Councillor Haacke left the meeting at 6:35 p.m. and returned at 6:49 p.m.

The Chair called for a brief recess at 7:13 p.m. Committee reconvened at 7:17 p.m.
