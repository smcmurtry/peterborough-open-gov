Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC16-021 Heritage Preservation Office Report

Jacqueline Irwin declared an interest specifically with File: Z1612, as she lives in close proximity to the subject property. She did not discuss or vote on the matter.

“CARRIED”

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC16-024 Martha Kidd Commemoration Expenditure Request

Moved by Councillor Pappas

That the PACAC approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC16-024, dated June 2, 2016 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

a)

That the PACAC approve an expenditure of up to $1,000 for coordination services in support of the continued development of the Peterborough hiSTORIES project as a part of honouring and celebrating the legacy of Martha Kidd in Peterborough, and

b)

That the Martha Kidd sub-committee continue to explore the requirements of digitizing her collections.

“CARRIED”

Councillor Pappas left the meeting at 7:57 p.m.
