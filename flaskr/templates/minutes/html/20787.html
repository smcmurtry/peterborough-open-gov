Moved by Councillor Clarke

That Council approve the recommendations outlined in Report PLPD15-007 dated February 9, 2015, of the Manager, Planning Division, as follows:

That Section 4.3.5 of the Official Plan be amended to add Section 4.3.5.8 Special Provisions for the lands known as 1840-1866 Lansdowne Street West, to permit up to one discount variety store with a maximum floor area of 950m2 in accordance with the draft amendment attached as Exhibit “C” to Report PLPD15-007;

That Section 3.9 Exceptions of Zoning By-law 1997-123 be amended by adding Exception No. 297 to include one discount variety store with a maximum floor area of 950m2 as a permitted use, in accordance with Exhibit ‘C’ attached to Report PLPD15-007; and

That the subject property be rezoned from SP.121 – Special Commercial District to SP.121-297 Special Commercial District in accordance with Exhibit ‘C’ attached to Report PLPD15-007.

“CARRIED”

Moved by Councillor Riel

That Council approve the recommendations outlined in Report PLPD15-005 dated February 9, 2015, of the Manager, Planning Division, as follows:

That Chapter 624 (Sign By-law) of the City of Peterborough Municipal Code be repealed and replaced with the Sign By-law amendment, attached as Exhibit ‘B’ to Report PLPD15-005.
