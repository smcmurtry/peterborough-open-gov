YMCA Redevelopment

Moved by Paul Lumsden

That PACAC accepts in principle the design for the redevelopment of 475 George Street North as issued by Atria Development Corporation for PACAC review on January 7, 2016, understanding that the 1909 gymnasium and 1932 addition will be demolished with the exception of the 1932 front entrance. It is the expectation of the Committee that it will review final permit drawings prior to issuance, for the purpose of approving the details of the final proposed alterations to heritage attributes.
