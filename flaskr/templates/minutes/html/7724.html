Other Business

Vision 2015: 10-year strategic plan for recreation, parks, arenas and culture: The Arts, Culture and Heritage Advisory Committee has been invited to attend the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee meeting at the Queen Alexander Centre from 5:30 - 7:30 p.m. on Tuesday, September 15th.
