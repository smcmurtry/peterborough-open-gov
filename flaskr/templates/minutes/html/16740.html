5. COMMUNITY UPDATES


5.1. John Martyn said Chris Kawalec, Coordinator for Peterborough Poverty Reduction


Network has been hired by the City of Peterborough as the Coordinator for the

Community Social Plan. The PPRN is looking for a Coordinator. John Martyn gave an

update on the Prince of Wales Neighbourhood Hub. Councillor Windover asked how

the County is involved with the Hub. John Martyn said that PPRN is in talks to

develop a similar hub in Norwood.

John Martyn reported that plans for the Food Depot are moving forward.


5.2. Linda Saunders said that CMHA is having a brainstorming session and she will


circulate the notes from that session. Linda Saunders suggested inviting the CMHA

Housing Manager to the next meeting.


5.3. Sarah Tate said that the Township of Smith Ennismore Lakefield has donated a lot to


Habitat for Humanity and is selling two other lots with the funds going into an

Affordable Housing fund.
