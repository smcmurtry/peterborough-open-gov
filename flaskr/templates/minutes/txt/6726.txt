Recreation Division Coordinator Parks and Forestry Supervisor Report APRAC16-001 Community Assistance – Beavermead Beach Volleyball Court Expansion
Moved by Councillor Haacke
That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report APRAC16-001 dated January 19, 2016, of the Recreation Division Coordinator, as follows:
That partnering with Soul Beach Volleyball and Invado Volleyball, through the Community Assistance Program, to establish two additional beach volleyball courts in Beavermead Park, at an estimated cost to the City of $25,500 be endorsed.
“CARRIED”
Recreation Division Coordinator Report APRAC16-003 2016 APRAC Meeting Schedule
