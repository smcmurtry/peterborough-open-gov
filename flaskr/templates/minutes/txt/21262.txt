Director of Public Works, County of Peterborough Update on Long Term Waste Disposal Environmental Assessment
Moved by Councillor Nelson
That  staff  be  directed  to  continue  to  communicate  with  Ministry  of  Environment staff  related the  County/City’s  commencement  of  an  Environmental Assessment  process  to  develop  a  long  term  waste  disposal  solution  for  the County and City, and further,
That  staff  do  not  proceed  during  2014 with  any  active  program  to  move  forward with  this  initiative,  but  to  report  to  the  Waste  Management  Committee  in  2014 regarding the value of commencing the process in 2015.
“CARRIED”
