Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC17-021 Adoption of Regulation 9/06 of the Ontario Heritage Act
Moved by Paul Lumsden
That the PACAC approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC17-021, dated May 4, 2017 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:
a)
That the current PACAC scoring system for determining heritage significance be replaced; and,
That regulation 9/06 of the Ontario Heritage Act, R.S.O. 1990 be adopted as the criteria for determining cultural heritage value or interest of a cultural resource by the municipality.
b)
“CARRIED”

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC17-022 Heritage Conservation Plan Funding
Moved by Paul Lumsden
That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report PACAC17-022, dated May 4, 2017 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:
That the Chair of the PACAC forward a letter to Council requesting that a capital budget allocation be included in the 2018 municipal budget for the completion of a Heritage Conservation Plan for the downtown.
“CARRIED”
Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC17-023 Heritage Preservation Office Report
