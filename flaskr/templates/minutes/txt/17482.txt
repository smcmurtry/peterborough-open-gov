Minutes of a Meeting of Museum & Archives Advisory Committee held

The meeting of the Museum & Archives Advisory Committee was called to order at 5:05 p.m. by Nancy Jackson, Chair, in the Fine Annex, Peterborough Museum & Archives.
Present: Janice Millard Nancy Jackson, Chair Linda Lumsden Morgan Tamplin Councillor Riel Gayle McIntyre (arrived at 5:12 p.m.) Karen Carter-Edwards Ann Sullivan Ken Brown
Staff: Allan Seabrooke, Chief Administrative Officer Susan Neale, Museum Director Mark Buffone, Accessibility Compliance Coordinator Kendra Sedgwick, Committee Coordinator
There was a welcome and introduction of Allan Seabrooke, Chief Administrative Officer for the City of Peterborough.

That the minutes of the Museum & Archives Advisory Committee meeting held on May 14, 2015 be approved.

Other Business
Susan Neale congratulated Janice Millard on her retirement from Trent University and offered thanks for her commitment to the Committee during the past few years.
Linda Lumsden commended Susan Neale and the Museum staff for the great work accomplished during the past year.
Susan Neale informed staff that the Museum was presented with the Community Partner Award of Excellence 2015 from Community Living Peterborough.
Ken Brown requested that an update report be provided at the October Committee meeting on the possibility of a Regional Archive.
