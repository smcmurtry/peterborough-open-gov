Director of Community Services Report APRAC15-016 Proposed Renaming of Simcoe and Bethune Park
Moved by Rocky Caravaggio
That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report APRAC15-016 dated March 17, 2015, of the Director of Community Services as follows:
a)
That the correspondence and presentation from Hank Fisher proposing to rename the Simcoe and Bethune Street Park in honour of Stompin’ Tom Connors be received;
That Hank’s proposal for renaming the park not be supported or endorsed;
That the matter of honouring Stompin’ Tom be referred to the Arts Culture & Heritage Division/Advisory Committee for consideration as either a potential public art project or other appropriate form of recognition; and
That staff be directed to establish a process to gather ideas / names for renaming the park and a report be brought back to the Committee outlining the process for receiving proposals.
“CARRIED”
