Recreation Division Coordinator Report APRAC16-028 Community Assistance – Cameron Park
Moved by Katy Shufelt
That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report APRAC16-028 dated September 20, 2016, of the Recreation Division Coordinator, as follows:
a)
That staff be directed to:
i)
Assist the representatives of the Cameron Park neighbourhood, to establish a Neighbourhood Association, with the goal of developing amenities within Cameron Park, through the Community Assistance Program; and
Report back to the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee once the Neighbourhood Association has been established and the details of a specific project are identified.
ii)
