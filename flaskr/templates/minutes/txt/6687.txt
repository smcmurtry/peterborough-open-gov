Minutes of a Meeting of Arenas, Parks and Recreation Advisory

The Arenas, Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee meeting was called to order by Gary Rabjohn at 5:30 p.m. in the Sutherland Room.

Staff: Ken Doherty, Director of Community Services Rob Anderson, Recreation Division Coordinator Sue Warrington, Arena Division Manager Phil Jacobs, Supervisor - Parks and Forestry Geoff Matthews, Manager, Peterborough Wellness Centre Kendra Sedgwick, Committee Coordinator

That the minutes of the meeting of the Arenas, Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee held on April 21, 2015 be approved.
“CARRIED”

Supervisor of Parks and Forestry Report APRAC15-028 Approve the removal of Humber Park swing set and re-install the swing set in Dixon Park
Moved by Councillor Haacke
That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report APRAC15-028 dated May 26, 2015, of the Supervisor of Parks and Forestry, as follows:
That the Public Works Division be allowed to remove the existing swing set in Humber Park and re-install the swing set in Dixon Park.
“CARRIED”

Recreation Division Coordinator Arena Division Manager Report APRAC15-029 Special Occasion Permit for 2015 Peter Robinson Festival
Moved by Councillor Haacke
That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report APRAC15-029 dated May 26, 2015, of the Recreation Division Coordinator and the Arena Division Manager, as follows:
That the issuance of a Public Event Special Occasion permit be endorsed for the Peter Robinson Festival occurring within Morrow Park from July 31 to August 2, 2015, in accordance with the Liquor Licence Act, and the City of Peterborough Alcohol Management Policy.
“CARRIED”
Director of Community Services Report APRAC15-030 Proposed Public Art Project for James Stevenson Park
Moved by Councillor Haacke
That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee endorse the recommendation outlined in Report APRAC15-030 dated May 26, 2015, of the Director of Community Services as follows:
That the information on the proposed public art project in James Stevenson Park, under the Hunter Street Bridget be received; and that the project be endorsed.
“CARRIED”

Recreation Division Coordinator Report APRAC15-026 Sport Kawartha Comparative Recreational Rental Rates Study
Ryan Minicola and Erik Monk, Sport Kawartha provided a presentation on Report APRAC15-026.
Moved by Councillor Haacke
That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report APRAC15-026 dated May 26, 2015, of the Recreation Division Coordinator, as follows:

Other Business
Councillor Therrien informed Committee members about the Stewart Street Playstreet event taking place on Sunday, May 31st from 10 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.
Geoff Matthews let the Committee know that the Peterborough Sport and Wellness Centre will be celebrating its 10th Anniversary on Tuesday, June 3rd and the speeches will begin at 11:00 a.m.
Adjournment
Moved by Diane Sargent
That this meeting of the Arenas, Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee adjourn at 6:30 p.m.
"CARRIED"
