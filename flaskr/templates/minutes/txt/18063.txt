Museum Director Report MAAC14-005 Peterborough Museum & Archives: Archaeology Policy
Moved by Councillor Pappas
That the Museum & Archives Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report MAAC14-005 dated April 10, 2014, of the Museum Director, as follows:
a)
At its meeting of April 10, 2014 Committee made the following amendments to the draft Policy:
b)
That the Archaeology Policy (draft), attached as Appendix A, be approved.
That the word ‘Collections’ be added to the title, changing the name to Archaeology Collections Policy.
In the second paragraph of Number 1 – Intent, the word ‘property’ be replaced with ‘responsibility’, so that it now reads “The Archaeology Policy works in conjunction with the City’s Archaeology Policy #0006.  It is understood that archaeological materials are the responsibility of the Contracted Licensed Archaeologist, unless otherwise agreed upon by the mutual parties.”
c)
Number 7 – Fees should have the following added: A box is not to exceed 30 pounds or 13.6 kilograms.
d)
“CARRIED”
