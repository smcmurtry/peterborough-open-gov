Disclosure of Pecuniary Interest
Councillor Pappas declared an interest with Report PACAC15-014, Approval of Verandah Restoration and Report PACAC15-011, Heritage Preservation Office Report as his parents live in proximity to the property at 1545 Monaghan Road.
Consent Agenda
No items were considered as part of the Consent Agenda.
The Chair moved to agenda item 5, Report PACAC15-014, Approval of Verandah Restoration.
Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC15-014 Approval of Verandah Restoration
Councillor Pappas, due to his previously declared interest did not speak to or vote on the matter.
Neil Campbell of Aside Architects and Andi vanKoeverden, Strategic Advancement Director of Mount St. Joseph Project made a presentation.
Moved by Kaes VanderKooy
That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC15-014, dated April 2, 2015 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:
a)
That the PACAC receive the restoration plan for the front Verandahs of 1545 Monaghan Road, the former Mount St Joseph convent and;
That the PACAC approve the plan, and;
