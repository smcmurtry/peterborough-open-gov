Minutes  of  a  Meeting  Of  City  Council  Held  On  July  2,  2013,  in  the
Council Chambers.

The City Council meeting was called to order at 6:30 p.m. in the Council Chambers, City Hall.
Roll Call: Councillor Beamer Councillor Clarke Councillor Doris Councillor Hall Councillor Juby Councillor McWilliams Councillor Pappas Councillor Parnell (7:59 p.m.) Councillor Riel Councillor Vass Mayor Bennett
Mayor  Bennett  noted  that  City  Clerk  John  Kennedy  received  his  Certified  Municipal Officer  designation  through  the  Association  of  Municipal  Clerks  and  Treasurers  of Ontario.
The Mayor commented on the late Gwen Brown who recently received the Senior of the Year Award. A tribute video of her life is available on the Mayor’s homepage.
