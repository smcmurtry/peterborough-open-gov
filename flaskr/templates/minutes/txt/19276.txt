The agenda was amended to move Item No. 5, Report PACAC15-009, Alteration Application 512 Weller Street, to be dealt with first.
Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC15-009 Alteration Application 512 Weller Street
Moved by Paul Lumsden
That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC15-009, dated March 5, 2015 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:
a)
That the PACAC receive an application for an alteration to 512 Weller Street, a property designated under the Ontario Heritage Act, and;
That the PACAC provide a decision, approving the application, based on the suitability of the proposed alterations, which is to include wood windows instead of vinyl casements, and
b)
That a letter containing the decision be provided to the property owner.

c)
“CARRIED”
