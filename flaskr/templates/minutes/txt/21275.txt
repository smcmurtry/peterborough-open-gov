Director of Utility Services, City of Peterborough Report WMC14-001 Mattress Recycling Proposal – Postponement of Decision
Moved by Councillor Nelson
That the Waste Management Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report WMC14-001 dated January 20, 2014 of the Director of Utility Services, as follows:
That a decision on the proposed implementation of a mattress recycling program at the Peterborough County/City Waste Management Facility (PCCWMF) be delayed until the next WMC meeting on March 3, 2014, allowing staff to ensure all affected parties have received and read notification about the proposal and have had an opportunity to comment.
“CARRIED”
Director of Utility Services, City of Peterborough Report WMC14-002 2013 Tonnage Summary for County/City Waste Management Facility
Moved by Councillor Pappas
That the Waste Management Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report WMC14-002 dated January 20, 2014 of the Director of Utility Services, as follows:
That Report WMC14-002 providing a summary of the tonnage received at the County/City Waste Management Facility for the years 2009 to 2013 inclusive be received for information.


“CARRIED”
