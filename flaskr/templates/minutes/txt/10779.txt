City Of Peterborough

May 14, 2012

Draft Minutes Not Approved




Minutes  of  a  Meeting  of  City  Council  Held  on  May  14,  2012,  in  the
Council Chambers, City Hall

Meeting called to order at 6:45 p.m. in the Council Chambers.
Roll Call: Councillor Beamer Councillor Clarke Councillor Hall Councillor Juby Councillor McWilliams Councillor Pappas Councillor Parnell Councillor Riel Councillor Vass Mayor Bennett
Absent: Councillor Doris
Ceremonial Presentations
Mayor Bennett proclaimed June 1-4, 2012 the Queen’s Diamond Jubilee Weekend and presented the following individuals from the Monarchist League of Canada, Peterborough Branch with a proclamation; Major Reverend Donald Howson, Marjorie Shephard, Walter Shephard, Betty Hinton, John Turner and Glenys Reid.
Councillor Parnell introduced the Be a Donor, Torch of Life Campaign.
Councillor Beamer introduced the City’s Youth Friendly Award and made a presentation to Mayor Bennett.
Councillor Beamer introduced the Mayor’s Youth Awards.  The Mayor presented certificates to Allison Ridgway, Madison Hargraves and a representative for Nicholas Degenais.
