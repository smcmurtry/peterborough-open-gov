Exterior Finishes Review 385 George St N
Report PACAC21-017
Moved by Dennis Carter-Edwards
Due to her previously declared interest, Deb Keay did not discuss or vote on the matter.
That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC21-017, dated October 7, 2021 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:
a)  That the PACAC seek consensus and provide comment on the proposed
exterior cladding for the development at 385 George St N,
b)  That the recommendation regarding the choice of exterior cladding materials
be forwarded to the Planning Division; and
c)  That the Chair send a letter to the Planning Division outlining the
Committee's concerns regarding the potential use of the Mitrex integrated solar panel on the building, as it may cause a safety concern for motor vehicles or pedestrians.
Carried




September Planning Circulations
Report PACAC21-018
Moved by Andre Vallillee

That the planning circulations received in September 2021 be reviewed for comment.
That PACAC approve the demolition of 512-514 College Street; and
That the designation by-law not be rescinded at this time.
