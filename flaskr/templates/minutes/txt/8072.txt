Other Business
The Public Art Advisory Committee (PAAC) currently has a vacancy.  An advertisement was published in the local papers, as well as the City’s website.  The PAAC Chair, indicated that volunteers from the Arts, Culture and Heritage Advisory Committee are needed to sit on the selection Committee.  Councillor Pappas, Dennis Carter-Edwards and Bruce Stonehouse volunteered to sit on the PAAC selection Committee.
