Minutes of a Arts, Culture and Heritage Advisory Committee meeting

The meeting of the Arts, Culture & Heritage Advisory Committee was called to order at 7:01 p.m. by Kendra Sedgwick, Committee Coordinator in the Sutherland Room, City Hall.
Present: Bill Kimball Bruce Stonehouse Councillor Pappas Councillor Parnell Dennis Carter-Edwards, Chair Dirk Verhulst Peer Christensen Bill Templeman

Staff: Ken Doherty, Director of Community Services Becky Rogers, Manager, Arts, Culture & Heritage Division Erik Hanson, Heritage Resources Coordinator Kendra Sedgwick, Committee Coordinator
Election of Officers – 2017
Chair
The Committee Coordinator called for nominations for the position of Chair.
Bruce Stonehouse nominated Dennis Carter-Edwards.
Councillor Parnell nominated Bruce Stonehouse as Chair.  Mr. Stonehouse kindly declined the nomination, but indicated he would be interested in continuing on as Vice Chair.
