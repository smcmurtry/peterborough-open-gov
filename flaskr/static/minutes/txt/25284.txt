General Committee meeting minutes of March 11, 2019
2019 Community Investment and Project Grants
Report CSD19-003
Moved by Councillor Akapo
That Council approve the recommendations outlined in Report CSD19-003 dated March 11, 2019 of the Commissioner of Community Services, as follows:
That the 2019 Community Investment Grants in the total amount of $209,500 be allocated to various local charitable and Not-For-Profit organizations (NFP’s) as set out in the attached Appendix A;
That the 2019 Community Project Grants in the total amount of $20,050 be allocated to various community groups as outlined in Appendix B;
That approved organizations be authorized and required to use the City Logo to acknowledge City support on promotional material such as flyers, brochures and posters throughout 2019;
That the current Community Investment Grant Review Committee be convened for a meeting as a focus group to give input on updating the application forms;
That a review of the process, eligibility criteria and financial reporting requirements for the Community Investment & Project Grants Program be undertaken in 2019 as requested; and
That the Peterborough Folk Festival be moved into the Service Grant Program, beginning in 2019, and that the 2019 allocation of $21,450 be funded through the uncommitted balances in the Community Investment and Project Grant Programs.
Carried
