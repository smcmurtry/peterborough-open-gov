Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC15-039 Creation of Walking Tours
Moved by Councillor Pappas
That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC15-039, dated November 5, 2015 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:
a)
That the PACAC undertake the production of two walking tours of the historic commercial core of the City, and;
That the PACAC fund two walking tours of the downtown to a limit of $2,000, and;
b)

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC15-040 Alteration Application 168 Brock Street
Moved by Martin Parker
That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC15-040, dated November 5, 2015 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:
a)
That the PACAC receive a presentation from Ken Trevelyan of Trevelyan Architect Inc. regarding an application for an alteration to 168 Brock Street, a property designated under the Ontario Heritage Act,
That the PACAC approve the proposed design, with the stipulation that the exterior finishes of the proposed addition be completed with natural or composite clapboard, and that metal clad wood casement windows be used, and that the property owner be encouraged to continue with the natural or composite siding for the remainder of the existing addition on the east elevation, and
b)
That a letter containing the decision be provided to the property owner.
c)
“CARRIED”
