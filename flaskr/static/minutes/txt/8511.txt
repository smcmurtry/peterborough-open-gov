Minutes of a Meeting of Arts, Culture & Heritage Advisory Committee
Held on March 14, 2013, in the Sutherland Room, City Hall

The meeting of the Arts, Culture & Heritage Advisory Committee was called to order at 7:02 p.m. by John Summers, Chair in the Sutherland Room, City Hall.
Present: Laurel Atkinson Dennis Carter-Edwards Fynn Leitch Councillor Pappas (7:07 p.m.) Councillor Parnell John Summers
Regrets: Michael Bell
Staff: Ken Doherty, Director of Community Services Becky Rogers, Manager of Arts, Culture and Heritage Division Brad Appleby, Planner, Subdivision Control and Special Projects Debra Soule, Municipal Cultural Planner Kendra Sedgwick, Committee Coordinator
Kendra  Sedgwick,  Committee  Coordinator  called  for  nominations  for  the  position  of Chair.    Councillor  Pappas  nominated  Dennis  Carter-Edwards  who  accepted  the nomination.  There being no further nominations, Dennis Carter-Edwards was declared the Chair of ACHAC for the remainder of 2013.
Nominations  for  the  position  of  Vice  Chair  will  de  deferred  until  the  April  ACHAC meeting.
