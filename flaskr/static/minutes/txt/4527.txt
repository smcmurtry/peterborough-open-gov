The meeting was called to order at 7:08 p.m.
Opening of Meeting
National Anthem - Peterborough New Horizons Band
The National Anthem was performed by the Peterborough New Horizons Bands.
Welcome and Opening Remarks - Mayor Bennett
Mayor Bennett provided introductory remarks regarding the Special Meeting of Council, which was arranged for the purpose of presenting the 2017 Civic Awards.
Civic Awards Format - Councillor Therrien
Councillor Therrien provided an overview of the various Civic Award Categories and explained the process that is followed to determine who will receive awards.


City Council minutes of June 5, 2018
Presentation of Civic Awards
Moved by Councillor Vassiliadis Seconded by Councillor McWilliams
