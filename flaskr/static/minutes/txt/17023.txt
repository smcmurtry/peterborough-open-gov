Library Manager/CEO Report PPL15-009 Retirement Gifts
Moved by Leslie Woolcott
That the Peterborough Public Library Board approve the recommendation outlined in Report PPL15-009 dated May 26, 2015 of the Library Manager/CEO as follows:
That the Peterborough Public Library Board approve Report PPL15-009 - Retirement Gifts, and directs the Library Manager/CEO to provide a choice of a mug with tea/coffee, a writing journal with engraved pen, an engraved bookmark, cut flowers, or a gift card, with cost not to exceed $25.00.
“CARRIED”
Verbal Report Library Foundation Update
Teresa Kerr provided an update on activities of the Library Foundation, and advised that the AGM will take place on May 27, 2015.
Moved by Mauro DiCarlo
That the verbal report of the Library Foundation be received for information.
“CARRIED”
Verbal Report Friends of the Library
Barb Connor provided an update on activities of the Friends of the Library, and it was noted that the $0.25 book shelf sells approximately 600 books per month.
Moved by Eric Prevost
That the verbal report of the Friends of the Library be received for information.
“CARRIED”
