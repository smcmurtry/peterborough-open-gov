Minutes of a Meeting of the Peterborough Architectural Conservation
Advisory Committee Held on June 5, 2014 in the Council Chambers,
City Hall.

The meeting of the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee was
called to order at 7:00 p.m. by Stewart Hamilton, Chair in the Council Chambers, City Hall.
Present: Catherine Dibben Councillor Pappas Stewart Hamilton, Chair Ivan Bateman Paul Lumsden Bronson Smith Kaes Vanderkooy
Regrets: Martin Parker
Staff: Erik Hanson, Heritage Resources Coordinator Kathryn McLeod, Heritage Researcher Kendra Sedgwick, Committee Coordinator
Minutes – May 1, 2014
Moved by Kaes Vanderkooy
That the minutes of the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee meeting held on May 1, 2014 be approved.
“CARRIED”

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC14-022 Heritage Hunt Funding Request
Moved by Paul Lumsden
That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report PACAC14-022, dated June 5, 2014 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:
That the PACAC approve the expenditure of $300.00 from the existing Heritage Hunt budget to reformat and print 500 copies of the Hunt as a fold out rack card to be made available at the Visitor Centre and other tourist locations.
“CARRIED”

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC14-023 PCVS Heritage Designation
Moved by Paul Lumsden
That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report PACAC14-023, dated June 5, 2014 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:
That the Peterborough Collegiate and Vocational Institute (PCVS), at 201 McDonnel Street, be recommended to Council for designation pursuant to Part IV, Section 29 of the Ontario Heritage Act as being of cultural heritage value or interest.
“CARRIED”
Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC14-011 London Street Generating Station Design Presentation
Moved by Councillor Pappas
That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC14-011, dated June 5, 2014 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:
a)
That the presentation by Eric Prevost, Generation Project Manager, Peterborough Utilities Inc., be received for information; and
That the Chair send a letter to Peterborough Utilities outlining the recommendations and concerns of the Committee, and request that information flow back to the Committee through the Heritage Office.
b)
“CARRIED”
Councillor Pappas left the meeting at 8:10 p.m.

Moved by Ivan Bateman
