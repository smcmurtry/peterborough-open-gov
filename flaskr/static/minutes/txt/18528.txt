Disclosure of Pecuniary Interest
Steve Guerin declared an interest in Item 3 (Report PACAC17-033 – Review of Heritage Conservation District Grant Applications), as he has family members who have applied for a grant.

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC17-032 Heritage Designation of 500 Gilmour Street and 503 Homewood Avenue
Moved by Paul Lumsden
That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC17-032, dated October 5, 2017 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:
a)
That the recommendation of the designation subcommittee be received, and;
That 500 Gilmour Street (Castlewraye) and 503 Homewood Avenue (the Bradburn House) be recommended to Council for designation pursuant to Part IV, Section 29 of the Ontario Heritage Act as being of cultural heritage value or interest.

b)
“CARRIED”
