Manager, Planning Division Report PLPD16-005 Downtown Economic Analysis (DEA) Update
Mike Johnson and Rowan Faludi of urbanMetrics Inc. made a presentation.
Moved by Councillor Pappas
That Council approve the recommendations outlined in Report PLPD16-005 dated February 8, 2016, of the Manager, Planning Division, as follows:
That the Downtown Economic Analysis Update, dated February 25, 2015, revised December 7, 2015, prepared by urbanMetrics inc., be received.
That the Downtown Economic Analysis Update be formally released as a Planning Study for the City of Peterborough to guide any refinements to municipal policies and strategies regarding the Central Area, in particular, the Official Plan Update and the Mayor’s Downtown Revitalization Report.
