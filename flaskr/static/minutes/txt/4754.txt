Report ACHAC18-018

At the meeting of June 28, 2018, Committee added the following:

That staff communicate to the PAAC members confirmation of the extension of their term to September 30, 2018 and request they acknowledge their willingness to continue, or verify they do not wish to continue as having completed their 2 year term as of May 31, 2018.
