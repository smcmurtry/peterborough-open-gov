Minutes of a Meeting of the Public Library Board of Trustees Held on
January 31, 2017 at the DelaFosse Branch, 729 Park Street.

The meeting of the Public Library Board of Trustees was called to order at 7:00 p.m. at the DelaFosse branch, 729 Park Street.
Present: Paul Stern, Chair Leslie Woolcott Teresa Kerr, Vice Chair Mauro DiCarlo Barb Connor Councillor Riel Councillor Baldwin Garth Wedlock Steven MacDonald
Staff: Natalie Garnett, Deputy City Clerk Jennifer Jones, Acting Library Manager/CEO Ken Doherty, Director of Community and Social Sevices (7:08 p.m.)

The Deputy City Clerk called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Peterborough Public Library Board for 2017.
Moved by Mauro DiCarlo
That Paul Stern be appointed as Chair of the Peterborough Public Library Board for 2017.
