Minutes of a Meeting of the Public Library Board of Trustees Held on
April 28, 2015 in the Library Board Room.

The meeting of the Public Library Board of Trustees was called to order at 7:00 p.m. by Paul Stern, Chair in the Library Board Room, 345 Aylmer Street North.
Present: Paul Stern, Chair Leslie Wolcott Barb Conner Mauro DiCarlo Teresa Kerr Councillor Baldwin
Regrets: Councillor Riel Eric Prevost
Staff: Jennifer Jones, Head Librarian Jennifer Zoethout, Library Manager/CEO
Guests: Ken Doherty, Director of Community Services Laura Gardner, Collections Development Librarian Mary Gallop, Manager of Facilities and Special Projects Peter Burton, VG+ Architects Nicole Crabtree, VG+ Architects
Minutes – March 31, 2015
Moved by Barb Conner
That the minutes of the Public Library Board of Trustees meeting held on March 31, 2015 be approved.
“CARRIED”

Disclosure of Pecuniary Interest
There were no disclosures of Pecuniary Interest.
Consent Agenda
No items were moved as part of the Consent Agenda
Verbal Report Presentation by VG+ Architects – Proposed Library Renovation
Peter Burton and Nicole Crabtree from VG+ Architects provided a presentation on the library renovation project including the background, process, design, budget and proposed next steps.
Moved by Leslie Wolcott
That the Public Library Board of Trustees support the renovation project as presented, and
That the library renovation plans be presented to Council in June 2015 and recommended for consideration as part of the 2016 construction budget.
“CARRIED”
Library Manager/CEO Report PPL15-005 Activity Report for March/April 2015
Moved by Councillor Baldwin
That the Library Manager/CEO proceed to purchase gifts on behalf of the Board for retirees in 2015; and
That a report on standardized retirement gifts be prepared for Board consideration.
“CARRIED”
