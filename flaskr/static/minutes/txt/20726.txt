Public Meeting Under The Planning Act Manager, Planning Division Report PLPD14-048 1474 Glencairn Avenue
Caroline Kimble, Planner, Land Use provided an overview of Report PLPD14-048.
Glenn Watt, 1468 Glencairn Avenue, spoke in opposition to the application.
Kerri Tojcic, 285 Towerhill Road, spoke in opposition to the application.
Councillor Juby left the meeting at 8:01 p.m. and returned at 8:05 p.m.
The applicants, Brittany Cadence and Trevor Digby of 451 Hunter Street West, spoke to the application.
Moved by Councillor Clarke
That the application for 1474 Glencairn Avenue be denied on the basis that the application does not comply with Zoning By-law provisions in section 6.25 which require the ceiling height for a dwelling unit in a basement be a minimum of 1 metre above grade for 50 per cent of the floor space and the Zoning requirement of two parking spaces per dwelling unit has not been met.  Further, there are no zoned duplex dwellings currently in the area.
“CARRIED”
