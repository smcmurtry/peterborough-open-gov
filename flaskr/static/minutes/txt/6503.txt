Accessibility Coordinator Report AAC14-003 Accessible Parking Concerns at Simcoe Street and George Street
Moved by Bob Geddes
That the Accessibility Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report AAC14-003, dated January 8, 2014, of the Accessibility Coordinator, as follows:
That the Accessibility Advisory Committee review and, provide recommendations to the Transportation Department regarding accessible parking issues at the George Street and Simcoe Street intersection.
“CARRIED”
Moved by Marie Bongard
That the Accessible Advisory Committee request that the transportation division staff investigate and provide a report on the possibility of turning the loading zone on George Street north of Simcoe Street to a 15 minute drop off zone, and if supportive, the report be forwarded to Council for approval.
“CARRIED”
