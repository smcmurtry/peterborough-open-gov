Director of Community Services Report ACHAC15-014 Update on Public Art Projects
Moved by Bruce Stonehouse
That the Arts Culture & Heritage Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report ACHAC15-014 dated April 9, 2015, of the Director of Community Services as follows:
a)
That a verbal update by Interim Public Art Committee Chair, Bruce Stonehouse, on Public Art projects be received; and
b)
