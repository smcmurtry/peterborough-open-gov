Recreation Division Coordinator Parks and Forestry Supervisor Report APRAC14-033 Community Assistance Projects

Moved by Councillor Beamer

That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee endorse the recommendations outlined in Report APRAC14-033 dated September 16, 2014, of the Recreation Division Coordinator and Parks and Forestry Supervisor, as follows:

a)

That repurposing the tennis court located within Knights of Columbus Park for use as a Pickleball court continues to be supported, in principle, pending a follow-up report from Staff in February 2015;

b)

That the estimated $34,000 (plus HST) representing the City’s share of the expense to construct a children’s play structure in Stewart Park be funded through the Community Assistance Program.

“CARRIED”
