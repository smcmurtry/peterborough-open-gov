Recreation Division Coordinator Report APRAC14-036 2014 Tollington Parks Endowment Disbursement

Moved by Councillor Therrien

That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report APRAC14-036 dated December 10, 2014, of the Recreation Division Coordinator, as follows:

a)

That staff be directed to submit a request to The Tollington Parks Endowment Fund trustees seeking their support to allocate the 2014 disbursement of the Tollington Parks Endowment Fund, in the amount of $73,898, to enhance the development of an accessible splash pad in the City’s west end; and

b)

That upon confirmation of support from the Tollington Trustees, Staff be directed to prepare a report to Council recommending the 2014 disbursement of the Tollington Parks Endowment Fund be allocated to enhance the west end accessible splash pad project.

“CARRIED”
