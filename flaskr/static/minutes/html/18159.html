Minutes of a Meeting of the Peterborough Architectural Conservation

Advisory Committee Held on June 4, 2015 in the Sutherland Room,

City Hall.


The meeting of the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee was

called to order at 7:00 p.m. by Stewart Hamilton, Chair in the Sutherland Room, City Hall.

Present: Stewart Hamilton, Chair Kaes Vanderkooy Paul Lumsden Jacqueline Irwin Councillor Pappas Martin Parker Catherine Dibben (arrived at 7:03 p.m.)

Regrets: Steve Guerin Ivan Bateman

Staff: Erik Hanson, Heritage Resources Coordinator Kathryn McLeod, Heritage Researcher Cassandra Coleman, Canada Works summer intern Kendra Sedgwick, Committee Coordinator

Erik Hanson informed the Committee that Kathryn McLeod was the successful candidate for the Heritage Researcher position. He welcomed her back to the position and Erik also introduced Cassandra Coleman to the Committee. She is the Heritage office’s summer intern and will be working on putting together the application process for alterations to designated buildings.

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC15-020 PACAC Strategic Plan

Moved by Councillor Pappas

That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report PACAC15-020, dated June 4, 2015 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

That the report with respect to the review of the Strategic Planning Exercise undertaken by the PACAC be received for discussion.

At the meeting, Committee added the following:

That a communications sub-committee be established, consisting of Martin Parker, Jacqueline Irwin and Councillor Pappas to investigate newsletter and website opportunities and report back at the September Committee meeting; and

That a sub-committee be struck, consisting of Catherine Dibben and Paul Lumsden, to prioritize the Strategic Plan initiatives and report back to Committee in September.

“CARRIED”
