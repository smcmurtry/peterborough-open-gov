i)

Lands consisting of approximately 3,376 square metres as shown in blue on the attached Appendix A and located on the west side of the Rotary Trail, be authorized for sale to the owner(s) of the lands adjacent to the eastern limit of the Rotary Trail for approximately $157,000 ($46.50 per square metre); and

ii) Land consisting of approximately 691 square metres as shown in red on
