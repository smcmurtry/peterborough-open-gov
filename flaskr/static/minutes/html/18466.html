Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC17-020 Alteration Application 583 King Street

Moved by Councillor Pappas

That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC17-020, dated April 6, 2017 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

a)

That the PACAC receive an application for an alteration to 583 King St., a property designated under the Ontario Heritage Act, and;

That the PACAC approve the application, based on the suitability of the proposed alterations, and;

b)

That a letter containing the decision be provided to the property owner.

c)

“CARRIED”

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC17-019 Cornerstone Award Nomination

Moved by Ivan Bateman

That the PACAC approve the recommendation outlined in Report PACAC17-019, dated April 6, 2017 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

That the PACAC nominate Paul Millard for an Ecclesiastical Insurance Cornerstone Award in the category of Adaptive Use/Rehabilitation for his adaptive reuse of Knox United Church.

“CARRIED”

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC17-017 Heritage Preservation Office Report

Next Meeting

The Chair indicated that the next meeting of the Committee is scheduled for May 4, 2017.

Moved by Jacqueline Irwin

That staff provide a report at the May Committee meeting recommending that the PACAC cease using its current evaluation process for determining cultural heritage value or interest of a property and adopt Regulation 9/06 of the Ontario Heritage Act R.S.O. 1990 as the criteria for determining cultural heritage value or interest.
