Recreation Division Coordinator Parks and Forestry Supervisor Report APRAC14-029 Converting Knights of Columbus Tennis Court to a Pickleball Court

Moved by Gary Baldwin

That the Rules of Procedure be suspended to permit Mr. Anderson to answer questions relating to Report APRAC14-029.

“CARRIED”

Moved by Dave Haacke

That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report APRAC14-029 dated June 17, 2014, of the Recreation Division Coordinator and the Parks and Forestry Supervisor, as follows:

a)

That repurposing the tennis court located within Knights of Columbus Park for use as a Pickleball court be approved in principle;

That staff be directed to work with the local Pickleball group to determine the most appropriate method and estimated cost to repurpose the facility, and to report back to the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee in September 2014; and

That the Pickleball court project be approved in principle as a Community Assistance project, pending the outcome of the actions identified in recommendation (b) of this report.

“CARRIED”
