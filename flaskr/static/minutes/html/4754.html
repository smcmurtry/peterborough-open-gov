Extension of Term of Public Art Advisory Committee

Report ACHAC18-018

Moved by Bruce Stonehouse

That the ACHAC approve the recommendations outlined in Report ACHAC18-018, dated June 28, 2018 Manager of Arts, Culture and Heritage, as follows:

That the term of the Public Art Committee be extended until September 30, 2018, and

That Staff be directed to prepare a report with recommendations for changes by which public art projects are identified and approved.

At the meeting of June 28, 2018, Committee added the following:

That the Committee recommends that representatives of ACHAC participate with staff in the development of the report to be presented to ACHAC at their November meeting.

That staff communicate to the PAAC members confirmation of the extension of their term to September 30, 2018 and request they acknowledge their willingness to continue, or verify they do not wish to continue as having completed their 2 year term as of May 31, 2018.
