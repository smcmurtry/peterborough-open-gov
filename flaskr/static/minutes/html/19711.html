Director, Planning and Development Services Report PLPD17-049 Removal of “H” – Holding Symbol 1657 College Park Drive

Moved by Councillor Pappas

That Council approve the recommendation outlined in Report PLPD17-049 dated October 10, 2017, of the Director, Planning and Development Services, as follows:

That the ‘H’ Holding Symbol of the zoning of the property at 1657 College Park Drive be removed by changing the zoning from the R.1, 1f, 2e ’H’ Residential District to R.1, 1f, 2e – Residential District in accordance with Exhibit ‘C’ attached to Report PLPD17-049.

“CARRIED”

Director, Planning and Development Services Report PLPD17-047 Removal of “H” – Holding Symbol from the zoning of 175 Hunter Street East

Due to his previously declared interest, Councillor Haacke did not discuss or vote on report PLPD17-047.

Moved by Councillor Clarke

That Council approve the recommendation outlined in Report PLPD17-047 dated October 10, 2017, of the Director, Planning and Development Services, as follows:

That the property at 175 Hunter Street East be rezoned from SP.356 – “H”- Residential District to SP.356 – Residential District in accordance with Exhibit “C” attached to Report PLPD14-047.

“CARRIED”

Public Meeting under The Planning Act Director, Planning and Development Services Report PLPD17-046 1600 Lansdowne Street West

Caroline Kimble, Planner, Land Use, made a presentation to Report PLPD17-046.

No one spoke in opposition to or in support of the application.

Due to his previously declare interest, Councillor Haacke did not discuss or vote on this item.

Moved by Councillor Clarke

That Council approve the recommendation outlined in Report PLPD17-046 dated October 10, 2017, of the Director, Planning and Development Services, as follows:

That the SP.215 – Commercial District be amended to add ‘a veterinary office (excluding boarding or kennel use)’, to the list of permitted uses in accordance with the draft amendment attached as Exhibit ‘C’ to Report PLPD17-046.

“CARRIED”
