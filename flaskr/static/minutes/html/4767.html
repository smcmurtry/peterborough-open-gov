7. Other

Naturally Occurring Retirement Communities (NORCs) The Community Development Program Manager informed AFPAC of discussions that took place in late March 2018 with Central East Local Health Integration Network to identify potential seniors housing sites in the City and County for funding to support the development of a NORC.

2019 Conference at Trent University In May 2019 Trent will be hosting “Aging Trent 2019”, a 3 day joint conference of the North American Network in Aging Studies (NANAS) and European Network in Aging Studies (ENAS). There will likely be an opportunity for Age-friendly Peterborough to be involved in some manner at this conference.
