Designation of 470 Water Street (County Courthouse)

Report PACAC20-029

Moved by Dennis Carter-Edwards

That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC20-029, dated September 17, 2020 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

a) That the report with respect to the designation of the County Courthouse be

received for information, and;




b) That the committee request that staff prepare a draft designation brief for

470 Water Street (County Courthouse and south wing addition) and the adjacent lands known as the County Jail, and Victoria Square.

At the meeting of September 17, 2020, Committee added the following:

c) The Chair provide a letter to County staff indicating that the Committee plans

to deal with this matter (tentatively) at the October 1, 2020 meeting.
