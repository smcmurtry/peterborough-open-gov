Heritage Resources Coordinator Report ACHAC14-014 Review of the Request for Proposal for the Former YMCA

Moved by Ivan Bateman

That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendation outlined in Report PACAC14-014, dated April 3, 2014 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

a)

That the RFP submitted in 2008 for the conveyance of the YMCA be received for information.

That all new development proposals received by the City of Peterborough be circulated to PACAC for approval, notwithstanding the existing approval.

That the PACAC Chair write a letter to the current owner of the former Peterborough YMCA building reminding that the PACAC Committee would need to review and approve any new design proposals.

“CARRIED”

Councillor Pappas left the meeting at 8:40 p.m.
