Director of Community Services Report CSD14-010 Community Investment Grants 2014

That Council approve the recommendations outlined in Report CSD14-010 dated May 12, 2014, of the Director of Community Services, as follows:

a)

That the 2014 Community Investment Grants in the total amount of $210,000 be allocated to various local charitable and Not For Profit organizations (NFP’s) as set out in the attached Appendix A.

That the Peterborough AIDS Resource Network (PARN) and the Peterborough Native Learning Program be moved to the Community Service Grant Program; and be awarded $7,000 and $3,000 respectively, with the funds to be drawn from the 2014 Contingency;

That approved organizations be authorized to use the City Coat of Arms to acknowledge City support on promotional material such as flyers, brochures and posters throughout 2014.

That all organizations requesting more than $10,000 through the Community Investment Grant Program in the future be required to provide an Audited Financial Statement, Notice to Reader, or Review Engagement, and that all other applicants be encouraged to provide the same or a copy of their financial statement presented to their Board and/or membership at their Annual General Meeting; and,

That those organizations approved for multi-year funding be required to complete a financial statement and program statistics annually for the duration of the funding period, to be reviewed by the Community Investment Grant Committee.
