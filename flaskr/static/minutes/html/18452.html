Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC17-014 Cenotaph Proposed Alterations Review

Moved by Kaes Vanderkooy

That the PACAC approve the recommendation outlined in Report PACAC17-014, dated March 2, 2017 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

That the PACAC determine its formal position on alterations to the Citizen’s War Memorial (Cenotaph) in preparation for discussions with the Cenotaph Advisory Committee.

At the meeting of March 2, 2017, the following was added:

That the Chair send a letter to the Chair of the Cenotaph Advisory Committee requesting that correspondence be provided to PACAC indicating the changes being proposed to the Citizen’s War Memorial (Cenotaph).

“CARRIED”

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC17-016 Heritage Designation of 144 Brock St. and 450 George St. N

Moved by Ivan Bateman

That the Peterborough Architectural Conservation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC17-016, dated March 2, 2017 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

a)

That 144 Brock Street be recommended to Council for designation pursuant to Part IV, Section 29 of the Ontario Heritage Act as being of cultural heritage value or interest; and,

That 450 George Street N. be recommended to Council for designation pursuant to Part IV, Section 29 of the Ontario Heritage Act as being of cultural heritage value or interest.


b)

“CARRIED”
