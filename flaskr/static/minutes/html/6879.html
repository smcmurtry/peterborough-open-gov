Recreation Division Coordinator Report APRAC17-016 Peterborough Canoe Club Presentation

Jim Stewart and Nicholas Paschalis, Peterborough Canoe Club, provided a presentation to Report APRAC17-016.

Moved by Glen Payne

That the Arenas, Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report APRAC17-016 dated May 16, 2017, of the Recreation Division Coordinator, as follows:

a)

That a presentation by the Peterborough Canoe Club’s executive members be received for information.

That APRAC support in principle the concept of the Peterborough Canoe Club (PCC) conducting programming on City owned land; and

That staff be directed to continue discussions with PCC, and other organizations as appropriate, such as but not limited to ORCA, Parks Canada, and Brown Sea Base, in determining an appropriate plan to meet the PCC's short and long term facility and programming needs.

c)

“CARRIED”
