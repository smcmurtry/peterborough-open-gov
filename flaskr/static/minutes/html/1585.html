Moved by Councillor Baldwin

That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report APRAC18-001 dated January 16, 2018, of the Recreation Division Coordinator, as follows:

a) That the community garden which was established in Nevin Park in 2016 on

a two-year trial basis be continued; and

b) That the Nevin Park Community Garden be expanded to a maximum of

twelve plots, and incorporate a pollinator garden.

Carried
