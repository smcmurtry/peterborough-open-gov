Minutes of a Meeting of City Council Held on October 1, 2012, in the

Council Chambers, City Hall


Meeting called to order at 6:30 p.m. in the Council Chambers.

Roll Call: Councillor Beamer Councillor Clarke Councillor Doris Councillor Hall Councillor Juby Councillor McWilliams Councillor Parnell Councillor Riel Councillor Vass Mayor Bennett

Regrets: Councillor Pappas

Ceremonial Presentation

Peterborough Lakers Brad Self and Chris White attended Council and presented the 2012 Mann Cup to Council.

Moved by Councillor Vass, seconded by Councillor McWilliams

That Council of the City of Peterborough confirm that the Peterborough Lakers' parade to be held Friday October 5, 2012 at 5 p.m., to celebrate their 2012 Mann Cup win, be considered a City-Sponsored Event.


“CARRIED”
