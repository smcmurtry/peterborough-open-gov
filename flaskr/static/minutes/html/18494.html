Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC17-024 Proposed Alteration 62 Dunlop Street

Moved by Councillor Pappas

That the PACAC approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC17-024, dated June 1, 2017 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

a)

That the PACAC receive a presentation from Ken Trevelyan of Trevelyan Architect, Inc. regarding a proposed alteration to 62 Dunlop Street (Malone), a property designated under the Ontario Heritage Act,

That the PACAC provide a decision, approving the application, with the condition that the final drawings submitted for permit, be approved by the Heritage Resources Coordinator; and

b)

That a letter containing the decision be provided to the property owner.

c)

“CARRIED”

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC17-027 City Hall Elevator Project

Moved by Martin Parker

That the PACAC approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC17-027, dated June 1, 2017 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

a)

That the presentation from Lett Architects regarding the proposed design for the City Hall elevator be received for information; and

That the PACAC approve the interlocking horizontal copper-siding, for the exterior material on the new elevator.

b)

“CARRIED”

Heritage Resources Coordinator Report PACAC17-025 Presentation: Liftlock Subdivision Development

Moved by Councillor Pappas

That the PACAC approve the recommendations outlined in Report PACAC17-025, dated June 1, 2017 of the Heritage Resources Coordinator, as follows:

a)

That the presentation by the City’s Planner, Subdivision Control and Special Projects, on the Liftlock Subdivision Development be received for information, and

That the Chair send a letter to the Planning Department indicating the Committee’s desire for the maximum buffer possible between the construction and the Liftock.

b)

“CARRIED”
