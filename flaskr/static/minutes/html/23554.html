Election of Chair


The meeting was called to order by Jennifer Jones, CEO at 6:00 p.m.

Ms. Jones called for nominations for the position of Chair of the Peterborough Public Library Board for 2019.

Dan Moloney nominated Leslie Woolcott as Chair and Ms. Woolcott accepted the nomination. There being no further nominations Ms. Woolcott was declared Chair of the Peterborough Public Library Board of Trustees for 2019.

Ms. Woolcott assumed the Chair.

Election of Vice Chair

Ms. Woolcott nominated Dan Moloney as Vice Chair, and Mr. Moloney accepted the nomination. There being no further nominations Mr. Moloney was declared Vice Chair for 2019.
