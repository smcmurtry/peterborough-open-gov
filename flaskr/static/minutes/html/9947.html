CITY COUNCIL MINUTES

CITY OF PETERBOROUGH


MAY 4, 2009


DRAFT MINUTES NOT APPROVED






MINUTES OF A MEETING OF CITY COUNCIL HELD ON MAY 4, 2009, IN

THE COUNCIL CHAMBERS, CITY HALL


Meeting called to order at 6:31 p.m. in the Council Chambers.

Roll Call: Councillor Doris Councillor Farquharson Councillor Hall Councillor Clarke Councillor Martin Councillor Peacock Councillor Vass Councillor Eggleton Councillor Peeters Councillor Pappas 7:36 p.m. Mayor Ayotte

Ceremonial Presentation

Bonnie Patterson, President of Trent University made a brief presentation regarding the contributions and attributes of youth in our community.

Councillor Eggleton introduced the award recipients.

Mayor Ayotte presented the Mayor’s Youth Award to David Bullock and Samantha Sherman.

Moved by Councillor Farquharson

That the rules of order be suspended (Article 24) to allow a special presentation to be made.

“CARRIED”

Move by Councillor Farquharson, seconded by Councillor Eggleton

That a special presentation be introduced at this time to award the Key to the City of Peterborough to Bonnie Patterson in recognition of her significant contribution to our community.

“CARRIED”
