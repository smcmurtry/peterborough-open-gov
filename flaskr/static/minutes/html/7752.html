Other Business

Canada 150 Committee ACHAC has been asked to recommend two individuals to the Canada 150 Committee. The Canada 150 Committee will select one of the nominees to sit on the Committee. ACHAC will nominate Andy Cragg and Bruce Stonehouse to the Canada 150 Committee.

Vision 2025 The Survey, which is available on the City’s website will close on November 15th. A Public Meeting will be held at the Evinrude Centre on November 25th. The Advisory Committees will have one more joint meeting in New Year to discuss Vision 2025.

Agriculture Society – Strategic Plan Agri-Food Synthesis is the successful consultant awarded the contract to work with the Agricultural Society to develop a Strategic Plan. The intent of the Strategic Plan exercise is to determine the future of the Agricultural Society and the activities at Morrow Park. Ken Doherty will keep the advisory committees updated during the process.
