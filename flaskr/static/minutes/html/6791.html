Arena Division Manager Report APRAC16-021 2016/17 Fall-Winter Ice Allocation Update

Moved by Councillor Haacke

That the Arenas Parks and Recreation Advisory Committee approve the recommendations outlined in Report APRAC16-021 dated June 21, 2016, of the Arena Division Manager, as follows:

a)

That the information on the allocation of prime-time ice to minor hockey groups for the 2016/17 Fall-Winter Season be received for information; and

That Staff allocate the available ten hours of prime-time ice for the 2016/17 season to the Adult User Groups who were displaced in the 2015/16 season, by offering the days and times that most closely match their original 2014/15 Fall Winter Season times.
