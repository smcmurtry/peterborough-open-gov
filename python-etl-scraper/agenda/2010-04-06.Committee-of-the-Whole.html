DETERMINATION OF ITEMS REQUIRING SEPARATE CONSIDERATION

4.

Director of Utility Services

Report USDIR10-002A

Comprehensive Transportation Plan Update –

Problem Statement / Transit Mode Share Target / Evaluation Framework

 

 

 

A report to recommend adoption of the Problem Statement, Transit Mode Share Target and Network Evaluation Framework for the Comprehensive Transportation Plan Update Project.

 

5.

Director of Utility Services

Report USPW10-001

New Municipal Works Facility – Location Study Results

 

 

 

A report to inform Council of the results of the new Municipal Works Facility Location Study results and to seek Council approval to site the new Municipal Works Facility (Public Works, Transit and Central Stores) on the municipal property at the intersection of Airport Road and Fisher Drive.

 

6.

Director of Utility Services Department

Report USEC10-007

Jackson Creek Detailed Flood Reduction Study

Approval to Post Project File

 

 

 

A report to advise Council of the Jackson Creek Detailed Flood Reduction Study Class Environmental Assessment, (the “Study”), and request that staff be directed to post the Study for the mandatory 30-day public review period in accordance with the Municipal Class Environmental Assessment process.

 

7.

Director of Utility Services

Report USTR10-002

Award of Proposal P-03-10 for Automatic Transit Fare Collection System

 

 

 

A report to recommend award of Request for Proposal (RFP) P-03-10 for the supply, delivery, installation, warranty, training and implementation of an automatic transit fare collection system for Public Transit.

 

 

8.

Director of Community Services

Report CSEM10-001

Ontario Power Generation Nuclear Emergency Preparedness

 

 

 

A report to recommend that the City provide Ontario Power Generation (OPG) with a Letter of Understanding, outlining the City of Peterborough’s proposed nuclear emergency planning initiatives for the period of 2010-2014 and to request financial support from OPG for nuclear planning, training, and drills.

 

9.

Director of Community Services

Report CSRS10-002

Community Gardens Policy

 

 

 

A report to request approval of the Community Gardens Policy and to inform Council of the process used to develop this policy.

 

10.

Director of Community Services

Report CSD10-007

Market Hall Performing Arts Inc. Agreement

 

 

 

A report to recommend an agreement with Market Hall Performing Arts Inc. (MHPAI) for a contribution to the renovation project at Market Hall.

 

11.

Director of Community Services

Report CSD10-008

Market Hall Renovation Project

Approval to Amend Agreements

 

 

 

A report to recommend certain sections of the Purchasing Policy be waived and that the Administrative Staff Committee be authorized to approve amendments to existing Agreements for the Market Hall Renovation Project within the approved budget.

 

12.

Director of Community Services

Report CSAD10-001

The Peterborough and District Farmers’ Market Association Agreement Extension

 

 

 

A report to extend the existing agreement between The City of Peterborough, The Peterborough and District Farmers’ Market Association and The Peterborough Agricultural Society for a period of eighteen months.

 

 

13.

City Solicitor and Director of Legal Services

Report OCS10-003

The Corporation of the Township of North Kawartha - Provision of Prosecution Services

 

 

 

A report requesting Council to pass a by-law authorizing the execution of an agreement between the City and the Township of North Kawartha, for the provision of prosecution services for parking infractions.

 

14.

Report CPFPRS10-014

Peterborough Airport ISF Major Infrastructure

and Airport Development Projects Status Update

 

 

 

A report to provide a status update on the Peterborough Airport ISF Major Infrastructure and Airport Development Capital Projects.

 

15.

CORRESP10-008

The Ontario Minister Responsible for Seniors

Senior of the Year Award

 

 

 

Correspondence from the Ontario Minister Without Portfolio, Responsible for Seniors, seeking nominations for the Senior of the Year Award, an award to recognize individuals who have enriched the social, cultural or civic life of the community.

 

16.

CORRESP10-009

The Ontario Ministry of Citizenship and Immigration

Lincoln M. Alexander Award

 

 

 

Correspondence from the Ontario Ministry of Citizenship and Immigration seeking nominations for the Lincoln M. Alexander Award, an award to recognize young Ontarians who have demonstrated exemplary leadership in contributing to the elimination of racial discrimination.

 

17.

CORRESP10-010

County Of Huron

Ontario Agriculture Sustainability Coalition

 

 

 

Correspondence from the County Of Huron regarding their resolution to support the Ontario Agriculture Sustainability Coalition (OASC) and the OASC lobbying for immediate adjustments to the Agri-Stability program.

 

 

18.

CORRESP10-011

County Of Huron

Septic System Maintenance Inspection Program

 

 

 

Correspondence from the County Of Huron seeking assistance from the Provincial Government for residential repair or replacement of rural septic systems.

 

19.

CORRESP10-012

The Métis Nation of Ontario

New Métis Council

 

 

 

Correspondence from the Métis Nation of Ontario regarding a community meeting to discuss the formation of Peterborough’s new Métis council.

 

20.

CORRESP10-013

Ontario Good Roads Association

Municipal DataWorks Project

 

 

 

Correspondence from the Ontario Good Roads Association seeking support of the Municipal DataWorks Project initiative.

 

21.

CORRESP10-014

Ontario Clean Air Alliance

Coal Phase Out

 

 

 

Correspondence from the Ontario Clean Air Alliance recommending, The Provincial Government place Ontario coal fired power generation plants on standby reserve for limited use only and to import coal-fired electricity from the United States as required.

 

Other Business

 

Adjournment
