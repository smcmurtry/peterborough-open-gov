Other Business

 

Councillor Pappas Intends to Present a Motion in Respect to Banning the Sale of Bottled Water.

 

WHEREAS the Federation of Canadian Municipalities has urged municipalities to mount a public awareness campaign regarding the benefits of drinking ordinary tap water;

 

AND WHEREAS municipalities such as London, Toronto, Ottawa, Vancouver and many others have already banned the sale of bottled water from their buildings;

 

NOW THEREFORE BE IT RESOLVED THAT staff create a report examining the possibility of similar action in the City of Peterborough.
