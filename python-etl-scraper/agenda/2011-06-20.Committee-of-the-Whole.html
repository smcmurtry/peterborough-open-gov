Report POL11-001

Request for City Council to support the implementation of a

Closed Circuit TV (CCTV) system in the downtown core

Presentation

 

 

 

The purpose of this report is to inform Mayor Bennett and Members of Peterborough City Council of the need to increase the number of CCTV cameras in the downtown core of Peterborough furthermore, to request support from the City of Peterborough to move forward with this proposal.

CORRESP11-012

Ontario Society of Professional Engineers (OPSE)

Future of Canada’s Nuclear Sector

 

 

 

Correspondence from the Ontario Society of Professional Engineers (OSPE) seeking the City of Peterborough’s support of OSPE’s five principles prepared for the federal Minister of Natural Resources that speak to the future of Canada’s nuclear sector.

 

22.

CORRESP11-013

Town of Amherstburg

Nuclear Reactor Program

 

 

 

Correspondence from the Town of Amherstburg seeking support of their resolution to oppose the shipment of radioactive materials through the Great Lakes to the St. Lawrence Seaway to Sweden by the Canadian Nuclear Safety Commission.

 

23.

CORRESP11-014

Town of Thessalon

Ground Mounted Solar Systems

 

 

 

Correspondence from the Town of Thessalon seeking support of their resolution to petition the Government of Ontario to reconsider the Green Energy and Green Economy Act 2009 to address the negative impact created by the provision for the installation of ground mounted solar systems.

 

24.

CORRESP11-015

United Townships of Head, Clara and Maria

Election Financial Reporting Requirements

 

 

 

Correspondence from the United Townships of Head, Clara and Maria seeking support to a resolution regarding the Municipal Elections Act to ensure that municipal representatives and candidates are held to the same standard as their provincial counterpart with regard to the penalties applicable for non-compliance to the election financial reporting requirements.

 

25.

CORRESP11-016

Peterborough Regional Health Centre (PRHC)

Ontario Heritage Trust – 2011 Community Recognition Program Nomination

 

 

 

Correspondence from the Peterborough Regional Health Centre (PRHC) seeking Council’s support of their nomination of the PRHC Heritage Committee volunteers under the Ontario Heritage Trust, 2011 Heritage Community Recognition Program.
