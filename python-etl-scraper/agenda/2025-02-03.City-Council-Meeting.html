Notice of Motion

 

14.a
Moved by Councillor Bierk, Seconded by Councillor Riel

 

Whereas over 60+ individuals in Peterborough are currently unsheltered, placing their health and safety at risk, particularly during the cold weather;


And whereas the existing shelter system, despite significant investment, remains insufficient to meet the immediate needs of the community;


And whereas municipalities, while constrained by limited resources, have a responsibility to respond urgently to humanitarian crises within their jurisdiction;


Therefore, be it resolved that:

  1. Council approve the allocation of $150,000 from the Social Services Reserve to support the creation and operation of temporary emergency shelter spaces, ensuring immediate protection for unsheltered individuals during the winter months.
  2. Staff be directed to work with community partners and agencies to implement this initiative as quickly as possible, identifying an appropriate location and ensuring necessary supports are provided.
